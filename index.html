Before you bring a pet home.

A beautiful website is nice. A prepared home is better. Use this quick checklist before you make the decision final.

Check your time honestly

Feeding, cleaning, play, training, grooming, and vet visits add up. Pick a pet that fits your normal weekday, not your most optimistic weekend.

Budget for care, not only purchase

Food, bedding, toys, vaccines, grooming, emergency vet visits, and boarding can cost more than the initial adoption or purchase fee.

Find a vet before you need one

For birds, reptiles, rabbits, guinea pigs, and fish, look for species-specific vet support before bringing the animal home.

Adopt when possible

Check shelters, rescues, and verified adoption groups. If buying, choose ethical, transparent breeders and avoid impulse purchases.
